Welcome to Outspread Wings Foundation, a non-profit organization dedicated to making a positive impact on our community and the world.
We come with a clear determination in mind to make a long-term impact on the society. Ideas can be free flowing; however, decision and idea execution is made with a clear mindset of sustainability.
At Outspread Wings Foundation, we believe that every individual has the power to create meaningful change, and together, we can make a difference. This organization is run by professionals who wants to give back to the society in whatever capacity and form possible. Our dedicated team of passionate individuals, our volunteers, donors, and supporters are at the heart of our mission, and we invite you to join us on our journey to make a difference and to create a better world for all.
Our Mission
Outspread Wings Foundation's mission is to create long lasting positive impact on the society. We are driven by our passion for supporting noble cause be it education, healthcare or upliftment of the underprivileged section of the society and our unwavering commitment of giving back to the society. We work tirelessly to achieve our goals through continuous execution and at the same time evaluation of its impact.
Our Work
1. School after School (SAS) – Education for underprivileged For those young champions who against all odds have not given up their dream and right to education, our foundation Outspread Wings extends support through our study center located at Jaipur, India. Students from different standards and schools from nearby locality participate in the classes run by our SAS program. This was established in August 2022 and since then around 50 children have enrolled to this program.
2. Practical Computer Training
At the same center, our foundation has setup practical computer learning center with required infrastructure for those children to be able to cope up with current education system. Practical computer classes are provided where computer basics are covered depending upon student’s age and grade.
3. Goodies
Every child love goodies, be it from a well to do section of the society or someone not so privileged like children at our study center. We make it a point to get a smile on faces of our little one by distributing some goodies each month.
4. Local schools support
Schools are the temple of education and foundation of the future of our upcoming generation. We at times participate in programs conducted at nearby local schools to motivate and support them. Enthusiasms of teachers and students motivates us to keep going.
